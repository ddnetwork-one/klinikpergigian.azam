Orthodontics & Braces

Straighten your teeth with our advanced orthodontic solutions.

01

Conventional Braces

Traditional metal brackets and wires. Conventional braces are highly effective in treating a wide range of orthodontic issues, from minor misalignments to severe bite problems.

02

Damon Braces

Damon braces are self-ligating braces that use a sliding mechanism instead of elastic bands. This design allows quicker adjustments and potentially less discomfort.

  • Faster treatment time
  • More comfortable
03

Damon Clear Braces

Damon Clear braces use clear ceramic brackets instead of metal, making them a more discreet option for patients who prefer less noticeable braces but still want to effectiveness.

04

Myobrace

Pre-orthodontic treatment designed for children. It focuses on addressing the underlying causes of crooked teeth, often without need for braces or extraction. It helps develop the jaw correctly.

Invisalign

The clear alternative to braces. Straighten your teeth invisibly.

01

Virtually Invisible

Invisalign aligners are made of clear plastic, making them nearly invisible. Most people won't even notice you're wearing them. Perfect for adults and teens.

02

Removable & Convenient

Unlike braces, you can remove your Invisalign aligners to eat, drink, brush, and floss. There are no food restrictions, and maintaining oral hygiene is much easier.

Professional Whitening

Over time, teeth can become stained by food, drinks, and lifestyle habits. Our professional whitening treatment is safe, effective, and provides results far superior to over-the-counter products.

Dental Implants

Permanent solutions for missing teeth that look and feel natural.

What are Implants?

A dental implant is a titanium post that acts as a replacement for the root portion of a missing natural tooth. It is placed into the jawbone and allowed to fuse with it over time.
